FAQ

Common LIC planning questions before you commit.

How much life cover should I plan for?

Most families begin with 12–15x annual income, then refine based on liabilities, children’s goals, and retirement dependency needs.

Can I combine tax-saving with long-term wealth growth?

Yes. A right-fit LIC portfolio can be structured to support tax efficiency, family risk protection, and long-term financial milestones together.

Do you help with claim support and nominee setup?

Yes. We support documentation readiness, nominee structuring, and claim guidance so your family is protected operationally, not just financially.

Can NRIs and global Indian families work with your team?

Absolutely. We support Indian-origin global clients through India-anchored, compliance-aligned LIC planning with structured advisory calls.

How quickly can my plan be activated after consultation?

In most cases, plan activation starts within 7–15 working days based on documentation readiness and underwriting requirements.

Will I get annual review support after onboarding?

Yes. Every active client receives periodic advisory reviews to ensure portfolio alignment with life-stage and goal changes.
